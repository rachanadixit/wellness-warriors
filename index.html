AUTOIMMUNE DISEASE

Mission

To raise awareness, educate the public on prevention and management strategies, and support individuals living with autoimmune diseases through impactful campaigns and resources.

Vision

To build a well-informed society that understands, prevents, and effectively manages autoimmune diseases, ensuring better health outcomes for all.

Objective

To spread awareness about the daily challenges faced by autoimmune patients, promote preventive measures, and empower individuals to overcome these conditions with informed care & support.

What is AUTOIMMUNE DISEASE?

Autoimmune diseases occur when the body's immune system mistakenly attacks its own healthy cells and tissues.
